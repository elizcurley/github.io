The Mangrove Method

Practical research, humane systems, artifacts that travel.

We help you make good decisions sooner by finding the smallest useful step—then building only what serves the decision.

About Elizabeth The Mangrove Method Origin Story

Principles

  • Smallest useful next step. Ship something true, then scale.
  • Decision-grade outputs. Clean analysis → plain language → a call to action.
  • Operational empathy. Systems fit the humans who use them or they don’t count.
  • Evidence with edges. We name limits, assumptions, and trade-offs explicitly.
  • Artifacts that endure. One-pagers, checklists, playbooks, dashboards—lightweight and reusable.

What this looks like in practice

  • Right-sized measurement plans that avoid busywork.
  • Logic models and SOPs matched to reality, not wishlists.
  • Implementation check-ins with clear, finite tasks.
  • Slide kits and briefs your team will actually share.
